Programming in R - Crash Course

This guide is aimed at the true novice, non-software engineer or data-scientist. 
We shall go from 0 comfortability in R to giving you the tools to get started and do work. 

I strive to avoid needless remarks, (such as this one!) so that your attention can be spent on R. I make two points here:
1: Many far superior R guides exist. Namely: https://r4ds.had.co.nz/ . My guide is a crash course. Additional guides are listed in the appendix
2: To the data-scientist, social-scientist, econometrician, psycometrician, or your flavor of epistemoligcal employment: 
With great computing power comes great responsibility, not to abuse statistics to lie.
